About AidBridge

Our Mission

AidBridge is a transparent donation and delivery tracking platform that bridges the gap between those who want to help and those who need it most. We connect Donors, NGOs, and Volunteers in a single, unified platform — making every donation trackable, verifiable, and impactful.

🎯

Transparency First

Every donation is tracked from pledge to confirmation. Donors know exactly where their items go.

🌍

Community Driven

Built around local communities — volunteers matched by pincode ensure fast, reliable deliveries.

🔒

Trust & Verification

All NGOs are manually verified by admins. Only genuine organizations are granted platform access.
