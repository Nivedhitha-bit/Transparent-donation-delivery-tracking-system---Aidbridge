How It Works

A simple, transparent, three-step process connecting everyone involved.

👤 For Donors

Donate in 3 Easy Steps

  • 1 Browse active NGO donation requests
  • 2 Click "Donate Now" and pledge your item
  • 3 Track your donation from pickup to delivery
🏢 For NGOs

Manage Everything in One Place

  • 1 Register and get verified by admin
  • 2 Post donation requests with specific needs
  • 3 Accept donations and assign nearby volunteers
  • 4 Confirm delivery and complete the cycle
🙋 For Volunteers

Deliver & Make an Impact

  • 1 Register with your city and pincode
  • 2 Get assigned to nearby delivery tasks
  • 3 Accept, pick up, and deliver donations
  • 4 Upload proof and complete the mission
